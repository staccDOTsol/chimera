⚠ Risk & safeguards

Use at your own risk. Bots running other bots' code is a real attack vector. Educational only — not built to stop bad actors, and we can't sus out who connects.

What protects you
• Ed25519 signatures + content-addressing — forged / tampered capabilities are refused
• Trust tiers — nothing runs from an author you didn't trust (default BLOCKED)
• node:vm sandbox — no require/process/network, 1s timeout
• x402 payment friction on METERED grafts

What doesn't
• node:vm is escapable by a determined payload — potential RCE on the host running a brain
• no vetting of who connects
• no defense against a determined bad actor
